High Protein Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular, versatile Asian dish made by stir-frying cooked, typically day-old rice in a wok or skillet with oil, soy sauce, eggs, and various ingredients like vegetables, meat, or seafood

Ingredients

  • Rice: 3 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old, cold)
  • Protein: 2 eggs (beaten), 200g chicken, shrimp, or tofu (diced)
  • Vegetables: 1 cup frozen peas and carrots, 3 green onions (sliced)
  • Aromatics: 3 garlic cloves (minced), 1 tsp fresh ginger (minced)
  • Sauce: 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Oil: 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Seasoning: Salt and white pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prep Ingredients: Make sure rice is cold and broken up. Beat eggs in a small bowl. Chop all vegetables and protein.
  2. Cook Eggs: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or large pan over high heat. Add beaten eggs, scramble until just set, then remove and set aside.
  3. Cook Protein: Add remaining oil, cook your protein (chicken, shrimp, or tofu) until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. Saut√© Aromatics: Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Fry Rice: Add cold rice to the wok, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until rice is heated through and slightly crispy.
  6. Add Vegetables: Toss in peas, carrots, and most of the green onions. Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  7. Combine and Season: Return eggs and protein to the wok. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. Toss everything together.
  8. Serve: Garnish with remaining green onions and a drizzle of sesame oil.
