High Protein Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a globally popular dish featuring long-strand spaghetti topped with a rich, slow-cooked meat ragù

Ingredients

  • Meat: 500g (1 lb) Beef mince (ground beef)
  • Vegetables: 1 onion (diced), 2 garlic cloves (minced), 1-2 carrots (grated/diced)
  • Tomatoes/Base: 1 tin (400g/14oz) chopped tomatoes or passata, 1-2 tbsp tomato paste/purée
  • Stock/Liquid: 1 beef stock cube or 250ml beef stock
  • Seasoning/Oil: 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, 1 tsp dried herbs (oregano or Italian herbs)
  • Pasta: 300g-400g dried spaghetti

Steps

  1. Sauté Veg: Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion, carrot, and garlic. Cook for 3-5 minutes until softened.
  2. Brown Meat: Increase heat to medium-high, add the beef mince, and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until fully browned.
  3. Add Sauce Ingredients: Stir in the tomato paste, canned tomatoes, beef stock, and herbs. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for at least 15-20 minutes (the longer the better for flavor).
  5. Cook Pasta: While the sauce simmers, cook spaghetti in a pot of boiling salted water according to packet instructions.
  6. Serve: Drain pasta and serve with the sauce on top, optionally garnished with parmesan cheese.
